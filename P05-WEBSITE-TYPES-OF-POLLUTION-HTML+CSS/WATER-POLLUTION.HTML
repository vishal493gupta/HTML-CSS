WATER POLLUTION CAN BE DEFINED AS THE CONTAMINATION OF WATER BODIE SUCH AS LAKES, RIVERS, OCEANS AND GROUNDWATER

CAUSES OF WATER POLLUTION

  • SEWAGE AND WASTE WATER
  • INDUSTRIAL WASTE
  • OIL POLLUTION
  • ACID RAIN
  • GLOBAL WARMING

PREVENTIVE MEASURES

  • SEWAGE TREATMENTS
  • PREVENT RIVER WATER FROM GETTING POLLUTED
  • TREATMENT OF WASTES BEFORE DISCHARGE
  • STRICT ADHERENCE TO WATER LAWS
  • ROUTINE CLEANING
  • REDUCING THE USE OF HERBICIDES AND PESTICIDES
