SOIL POLLUTION IS THE DEGRADATION OF THE NATURAL ENVIRONMENT OF SOIL BY VARIOUS POLLUTANTS. THESE POLLUTANTS INCLUDE PESTICIDES, INSECTICIDES, INDUSTRIAL AND HOUSEHOLD WASTE, LEAD FROM BATTERIES, NUCLEAR WASTE AND SEWAGE SLUSH.

CAUSES OF SOIL POLLUTION

  • DEFORESTATION
  • DUMPING OF WASTE
  • OIL LEAKS
  • INDUSTRIAL ACTIVITIES
  • LEAKING SEWERS, UNDERGROUND TANKS AND UNDERGROUND PIPELINES

PREVENTIVE MEASURES

  • FOREST IMPLANTATION
  • WEED CONTROL
  • REUSE AND RECYCLE
  • PROPER SOLID WASTE TREATMENT
  • PROMOTE USE OF BIO PESTICIDES AND FUNGICIDES
