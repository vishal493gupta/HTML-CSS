AIR POLLUTION REFERS TO THE RELEASE OF POLLUTANTS INTO THE AIR THAT ARE DETRIMENTAL TO HUMAN HEALTH AND THE PLANET AS A WHOLE.

CAUSES OF AIR POLLUTION

  • BURNING OF FOSSILS FUELS
  • AGRICULTURAL ACTIVITIES
  • EXHAUST FROM FACTORIES AND INDUSTRIES
  • MINING OPERATIONS
  • INDOOR AIR POLLUTION

PREVENTIVE MEASURES

  • REDUCE THE AIR POLLUTION OF YOUR AUTOMOBILE
  • USE ECO-FRIENDLY PRODUCTS IN YOUR HOME
  • PLANT TREES
  • KEEP AIR-CLEANING HOUSEPLANTS
  • TAKE A GOOD CARE OF YOUR FIREPLACE
  • RECYCLE AND BUY RECYCLED PRODUCTS
